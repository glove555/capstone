Tool: pK to Amino Acid



Take pKa of side chains and return amino acid match. Please be sure that chains being added together are closest in value. For example, if my unknown amino acid had a pKa of 2, 4, and 8. The pKas of the chains with 2 and 4 would be added and divided to form the isoelectric point. Be sure that your inputs are numbers or the program simply will not work :(
